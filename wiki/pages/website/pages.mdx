
# Website Structure

The Next.js Application Router layout.

## Page Hierarchy

- **`/` (Root)**: The landing page.
    - `components/hero-section.tsx`
    - `components/features-grid.tsx`
- **`/faq`**: Frequently Asked Questions.
- **`/privacy`**: Legal privacy policy.
- **`/terms`**: Terms of service.

## Components

### `Navbar`
The responsive top navigation. It manages the "Get App" CTA and smooth scrolling to sections.

### `Footer`
Contains legal links, social icons, and copyright info.

### `AppShowcase`
A 3D mock of the mobile app (using static images for performance) to demonstrate key features.
