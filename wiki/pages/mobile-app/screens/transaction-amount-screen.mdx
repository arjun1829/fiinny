# Transaction Amount Screen

High-level cashflow analysis.

## Overview

Compares the **volume** (monetary value) of Income vs Expense over time.

## Features

- **Cashflow Chart**: Stacked/Grouped bar chart showing total inflow vs outflow per time bucket.
- **Net Calculation**: Instantly displays total `Credit` and `Debit` sums for the selected period.
- **Visuals**: Uses distinct colors (Mint Green for Credit, Red/Orange for Debit) to highlight net positions.
