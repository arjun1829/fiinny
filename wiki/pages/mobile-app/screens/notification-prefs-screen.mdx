# Notification Prefs Screen

Fine-grained control over the app's push notification strategy.

## Overview

Allows users to tune "Fiinny's Voice" â€” deciding when and why the app should nudge them. It emphasizes high-signal alerts over spam.

## Features

- **Master Toggle**: Enable/Disable all push notifications (integrates with `PushService` permissions).
- **Channel Config**: Granular toggles for:
    - **Review Rhythm**: Daily reminders, Weekly digests.
    - **Brain Alerts**: Overspend, Loan watch, Goal milestones.
    - **Collaboration**: Partner check-ins, Settle up nudges.
- **Quiet Hours**: Time-picker based "Do Not Disturb" window, enforced by cloud functions or local scheduling.
- **Test Mode**: "Test nudge" button to verify push delivery locally.

## Architecture

- **Service Layer**: Reads/Writes to `NotifPrefsService` (Firestore).
- **UI**: Uses `_GlassCard` and adaptive switches for a premium feel.

None.
