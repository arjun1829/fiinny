# Credit Card Details Screen

Manages credit card cycles and bill payments.

## Overview

Lists all linked credit cards and their current billing status.

## Features

- **Due Calculation**: Uses `CreditCardModel.daysToDue()` to show urgency ("Due in 5d" or "Overdue").
- **Cycle Tracking**: Fetches the latest `CreditCardCycle` to display "Total Due" and "Paid Amount".
- **Mark Paid**: Long-press action to manually record a full payment for the current cycle.
- **Smart Notifications**: Integrates with `CardDueNotifier` to schedule local notifications for upcoming bills.
