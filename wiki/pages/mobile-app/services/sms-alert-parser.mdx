# SMS Alert Parser

**Path**: `lib/services/sms/sms_alert_parser.dart`

A wrapper around `CommonRegex` specialized for SMS formatting.

## Responsibilities
*   **`looksFinancial`**: Fast regex check for keywords (`bank`, `upi`, `debited`).
*   **`parse`**:
    *   Extracts amount (paise).
    *   Detects direction (Dr/Cr).
    *   Extracts Merchant ID/Name.
    *   Builds an idempotency key.
    *   Returns a `ParsedTransaction` object.

It serves as the "Adapter" converting raw SMS strings into the application's standard `ParsedTransaction` model.
