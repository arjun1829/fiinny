# Settings Service (`settings_service.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/services/settings_service.dart`

## 1. Overview
A simple Key-Value store backed by Firestore.

## 2. Logic
Stores data in a map field `settings` within the `users/{uid}` document.
- `setString(key, value)`: Merges the key into the settings map.
- `getString(key)`: Retrieves it.

## 3. Usage
Used for syncing simple preferences like "Default Currency" or "Theme Mode" across devices.
