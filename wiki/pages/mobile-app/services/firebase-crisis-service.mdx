# Firebase Crisis Service

**Path**: `lib/services/firebase_crisis_service.dart`

Manages the **"Survival Mode"** (Crisis Mode) state.

## Responsibilities
*   **Persist Plan**: Saves the user's crisis settings (`weeklyLimit`, `isActive`) to `survival_mode/{userId}`.
*   **Fetch Plan**: Retrieves the current plan.

## Context
Survival Mode is a feature where users can lock down their spending to a strict weekly limit during financial emergencies.
