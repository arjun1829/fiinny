# Card Meta Service

**Path**: `lib/services/card_meta_service.dart`

Manages metadata associated with credit cards, particularly their **limits**.

## Responsibilities
*   Stores `creditLimitInr` and `availableLimitInr` for a given card (identified by `last4`).
*   Path: `users/{userId}/cards_meta/{last4}`.

## Usage
Used by the Credit Card screen or parsing services to update available limits when extracted from SMS (e.g., "Available Limit: 50,000").
