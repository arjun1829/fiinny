# Fee Detector

**Path**: `lib/services/enrich/fee_detector.dart`

Extracts hidden fees and tax breakups from invoice text (parsing emails/receipts).

## Detects
*   Platform Fees
*   Convenience Fees
*   Fuel Surcharge
*   FX Markup
*   Late Fees
*   GST/Taxes

Returns a `FeeBreakup` object with a map of detected components.
