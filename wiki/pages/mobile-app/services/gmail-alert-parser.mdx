# Gmail Alert Parser

**Path**: `lib/services/gmail/gmail_alert_parser.dart`

Specializes in parsing **email alerts** from known banks.

## Responsibilities
*   **Whitelist**: `allowedFrom` regex limits parsing to official bank domains (`alerts@hdfcbank.net`, `noreply@paytm.com`, etc.) to prevent spoofing.
*   **Extraction**:
    *   Combines `Subject` + `Snippet`.
    *   Uses `CommonRegex` to extract financial details.
    *   Detects if the email is a simple P2P transfer or a merchant purchase.
*   **Metadata**: Retains the `gmailId` for deep linking or debugging.
