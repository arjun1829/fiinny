# Notification Service (`notification_service.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/services/notification_service.dart`

## 1. Overview
Manages **Local Push Notifications** using `flutter_local_notifications`.

## 2. Capabilities
- **Instant Alerts**: `showNotification` for immediate feedback (e.g., "Insight Generated").
- **Scheduled Alerts**: `scheduleAt` for future reminders (e.g., Loan Due Date).
- **Monthly Wrap**: Logic to schedule a "Monthly Wrap is ready" alert on the 1st of the month at 9 AM.
- **Channels**: Sets up Android Notification Channels (Importance Max).

## 3. Usage
Used by `FiinnyBrainService`, `SmartNudgeEngine`, and `LoanService` (for reminders).
