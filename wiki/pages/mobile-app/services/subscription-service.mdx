# Subscription Service (`subscription_service.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/services/subscription_service.dart`

## 1. Overview
Manages recurring payments (`SubscriptionItem`) like Netflix, Gym, or Rent.

## 2. Responsibilities
- **Scheduling**: `calculateNextDueDate` contains the core logic for determining when the next payment event occurs given a frequency (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly, Custom).
- **CRUD**: Standard Firestore operations for subscription items.

## 3. Usage
Used to populate the **Subscriptions List** and to generate **Recurring Transaction** suggestions.
