# Voice Bridge

**Path**: `lib/services/voice_bridge.dart`

Handles **App Links (Deep Links)** for voice commands (Siri / Google Assistant).

## Responsibilities
*   **Initialization**: Listens to `AppLinks().uriLinkStream`.
*   **Routing**:
    *   `fiinny://add-expense`: Opens the Add Expense screen.
    *   `fiinny://ask`: Logic for future "Ask AI" feature.

## Usage
Allows users to say "Hey Google, add expense on Fiinny" (if the shortcuts/intents are configured to open this URL).
