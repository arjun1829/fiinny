
# App Architecture

High-level overview of the Fiinny mobile application architecture.

## Principles

- **Framework**: Flutter
- **State Management**: Provider / Riverpod (Transitioning)
- **Navigation**: GoRouter
- **Pattern**: MVVM-ish (Screen -> Service -> Model)

## Layers

1.  **UI Layer**: Screens and Widgets in `lib/screens` and `lib/widgets`.
2.  **Service Layer**: Business logic in `lib/services`.
3.  **Data Layer**: Firestore models in `lib/models`.
