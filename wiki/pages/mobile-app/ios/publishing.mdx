
# App Store Publishing

Guidelines for submitting Fiinny to the Apple App Store.

## Privacy Manifest
As of Spring 2024, Apple requires a Privacy Manifest (`PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`) for apps and strict declarations for:
- User Defaults
- Disk Space
- Boot Time

Fiinny declares usage for **User Defaults** (storing theme/locale preferences).

## Screenshots
Screenshots must be taken on:
- 6.5" Display (iPhone 11 Pro Max / XS Max)
- 5.5" Display (iPhone 8 Plus)
- 12.9" Display (iPad Pro - optional if universal)

## TestFlight
1.  **Build**: `flutter build ipa`
2.  **Upload**: Use Transporter app (macOS) to upload `.ipa`.
3.  **Groups**: "Internal Testers" get updates immediately.
