
# iOS Permissions

Deep dive into `Info.plist` keys.

## Critical Keys

iOS requires explicit justification for accessing sensitive data.

### `NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Fiinny needs access to your photos to attach receipts to expenses."
*   **Trigger:** User taps "Add Receipt" -> "Choose from Gallery".

### `NSCameraUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Fiinny uses the camera to scan physical receipts."
*   **Trigger:** User taps "Add Receipt" -> "Take Photo".

### `NSFaceIDUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Authenticate to unlock Fiinny."
*   **Trigger:** App launch if "App Lock" is enabled.

## Background Modes

Fiinny uses **Background Fetch** to keep widgets up to date, but does **not** rely on extensive background processing on iOS due to system limitations.
