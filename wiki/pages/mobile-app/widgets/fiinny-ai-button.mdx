# Fiinny AI Button

**Path:** `lib/widgets/fiinny_ai_button.dart`

The entry point to the AI insights experience.

## Visuals
*   **Animations:** Contains a perpetually spinning sparkle icon (`AnimationController` repeating every 4 seconds) to draw attention.
*   **Design:** Elevated capsule shape with a teal border and shadow.

## Behavior
*   **Navigation:** Pushes the `/insights` route on tap, passing the `userPhone` as an argument.

## Usage

```dart
FiinnyAiButton(
  userPhone: currentUser.phone,
)
```
