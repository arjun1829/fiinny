# Glass Card

**Path:** `lib/widgets/glass_card.dart`

A core design primitive used throughout the app to create the "Fiinny Glass" effect.

## Implementation
*   **BackdropFilter:** Applies `ImageFilter.blur(sigmaX: 14, sigmaY: 14)` to content behind the card.
*   **Adaptive Color:** Defaults to `Colors.white.withOpacity(0.13)` in dark mode and `Colors.black.withOpacity(0.05)` in light mode.
*   **Border & Shadow:** Adds a subtle white border and localized shadow to lift the card from the background.

## Usage

```dart
GlassCard(
  borderRadius: 24,
  child: Text("I look frosty!"),
)
```
