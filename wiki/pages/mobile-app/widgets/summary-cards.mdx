# Summary Cards

**Path:** `lib/widgets/summary_cards.dart`

A row of three cards displaying the user's high-level social debt status: "You Owe", "Owed To You", and "Net Balance".

## Data Source
*   **Real-time Stream:** Listens to `BalanceService().streamUserBalances(uid)` to update UI instantly when a settlement or expense occurs.
*   **Color Coding:**
    *   **Red:** You Owe / Negative Net
    *   **Green:** Owed To You
    *   **Blue:** Positive Net

## Usage

```dart
SummaryCards(userId: currentUser.uid)
```
