# Group Model (`group_model.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/models/group_model.dart`

## 1. Overview
Represents a group of people for splitting expenses (e.g., "Goa Trip" or "Flatmates").

## 2. Responsibilities
- **Membership:** `memberPhones` list containing E.164 phone numbers of all participants.
- **Metadata:** Stores `memberAvatars` and `memberDisplayNames` maps to avoid fetching full profiles for every list item.
- **Creator Logic:** `GroupModel.withCreator` factory ensures the creator is always added to the member list automatically.

## 3. Usage
- **Collections:** `groups` collection.
- **UI:** `GroupDetailScreen`.
