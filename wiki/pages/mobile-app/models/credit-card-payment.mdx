# Credit Card Payment (`credit_card_payment.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/models/credit_card_payment.dart`

## 1. Overview
Tracks a **repayment** made towards a credit card bill.

## 2. Responsibilities
- **Source of Truth:** Defines *how* the bill was paid (`source`: 'gmail', 'sms', or 'manual').
- **Reference:** `ref` can hold the UPI transaction ID or Bank Reference Number for reconciliation.

## 3. Usage
- **Reconciliation:** Used to mark a `CreditCardCycle` as "PAID".
