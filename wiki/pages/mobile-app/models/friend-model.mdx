# Friend Model (`friend_model.dart`)

**Path:** `lib/models/friend_model.dart`

## 1. Overview
Represents a friend for social expense splitting.

## 2. Responsibilities
- **Identity:** `name` and `phone`. The **phone number** is the strict unique identifier.
- **Linkage:** Can optionally store a `docId` if retrieved from Firestore.
